**Date:** November 2025; Bilbao, Spain  

Finalist for the **Aula Inetum - Universidad de Deusto BSc/MSc Thesis Awards**, among the 5 students selected for my projects:  
- Built a portable embedded system with Raspberry Pi to sensorize an electric bicycle.  
- Developed a web application to monitor measurements and extract relevant temporal data.  
- Simulated a system of several bikes for scalability.

- At the university, I had received back in June a grade of 9.8/10 for my Computer Engineering thesis and 9.3/10 for my Industrial Electronics & Automation Engineering thesis.
